Tanakh character index

Search and filter the recurring figures across Torah, Nevi'im, and Ketuvim — by book, section, role, tribe, family, or non-blood lineage (mentorships, priestly succession, prophetic anointing). Switch to the timeline view to see how characters relate across generations and eras.

How to read this: eras follow the Tanakh's own narrative order, not a claim about historical dating. Within an era, characters are ordered as they appear in the story, so you can see who is a contemporary of whom — and threads like Judah → Boaz → David → Solomon show how a single family line runs across several eras and hundreds of narrated years.
